Visual Style
Realism Track
R1
Cinematic Realism
Anamorphic lenses, film grain, muted midtones, crushed blacks, motivated practical lighting.
Blade Runner 2049 · Arrival · The Social Network
R2
UGC / Native Content
Handheld micro-shake, no color grade, subjects look real not cast. Feels like you found it.
iPhone ProRes · BeReal · Raw TikTok drafts
R3
Documentary
Observational, natural light, talking-head framing, long takes, subtle desaturation.
Free Solo · Honeyland · Vice shorts
R4
Noir / Neo-Noir
High contrast, deep shadows, near-monochrome with one accent color, wet surfaces, 2am energy.
Sin City · Drive · True Detective S1
Period / World Track
W1
Historical / Period
Era-accurate wardrobe, film burns, decade-locked color palette.
Peaky Blinders · 1917 · The Crown
W2
Cyberpunk / Neo-Future
Neon darkness, chromatic aberration, teal and magenta, holographic UI.
Ghost in the Shell · Akira · Altered Carbon
W3
Black Mirror / Near-Future
Clinical, desaturated, sterile white surfaces. Technology beautiful and threatening.
Black Mirror S3 · Ex Machina · Her
Animation / Art Track
A1
Disney / Pixar 3D
Subsurface skin lighting, expressive eyes, saturated palette, smooth 24fps.
Soul · Turning Red · Encanto
A2
Studio Ghibli
Watercolor washes, hand-painted texture, lush environment, weighted movement.
Spirited Away · Mononoke · The Wind Rises
A3
Anime
Speed lines, impact frames, cel-shaded, dramatic lighting shifts on emotional beats.
Demon Slayer · Jujutsu Kaisen · Your Name
A4
Picasso / Cubist
Fragmented geometry, bold flat color, black outlines, figures deconstructed expressively.
Guernica · Gorillaz videos · Abstract MV
A5
Motion Graphics
Vector shapes, typography in motion, 3-4 color values, info reveals on beat.
Apple reveals · Stripe animated · Kurzgesagt
Director Mode Track
D1
Nolan Mode
Practical scale, IMAX composition, non-linear tension, cold blues + amber warmth for humans.
Interstellar · Dunkirk · The Dark Knight
D2
Fincher Mode
Cold, precise, clinical. No handheld. Desaturated green-grey. Information withheld.
Fight Club · Gone Girl · The Social Network
D3
Wes Anderson Mode
Perfect symmetry, pastel palette + one accent, flat deadpan, whip-pan transitions.
Grand Budapest Hotel · Asteroid City
D4
A24 Mode
Intimate, uncomfortable. Handheld but controlled. Natural light pushed to extremes.
Midsommar · Moonlight · Lady Bird
